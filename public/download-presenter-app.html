Installation Instructions

Follow these steps to install the Presentation Player:

  1. Download the installer for your operating system:
    Windows macOS Linux
  2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. After installation, the Presentation Player app should appear in your applications list.
  4. Start the app and log in with your client credentials to view presentations.
Note: After installation, clicking "Start Presentation" buttons in the dashboard will automatically open the Presentation Player.
